# 🧩 Blueprint Prototype Texture Addon (Godot)

A lightweight addon for Godot that provides **7 prototype textures (.png)** and **7 ready-to-use materials (.tres)**.  
Perfect for rapid prototyping, blockouts, and blueprint-style visualization in your projects.

---

## 📂 Contents

- **texture_01_white.png / .tres** — Minimal white base texture  
- **texture_01_blue.png / .tres** — Blueprint-style blue variant  
- **texture_02_white.png / .tres** — Alternate white prototype texture  
- **texture_02_blue.png / .tres** — Blueprint-style blue variant  
- **texture_03_white.png / .tres** — Third white prototype texture  
- **texture_03_blue.png / .tres** — Blueprint-style blue variant  
- **texture_04.png / .tres** — Unique standalone texture  

---

## 🎨 Features

- **Rapid prototyping**: Quickly block out levels with consistent visuals.  
- **Dual color scheme**: White for neutral placeholders, blue for blueprint clarity.  
- **Plug-and-play materials**: `.tres` files are ready to drop into your scenes.  
- **Scalable workflow**: Ideal for iterating on design before final art assets.  

---

## 🚀 Installation

1. Copy the addon folder into your Godot project’s  directory.   
2. Access the textures and materials directly from the **FileSystem** tab.  

---

## 🛠 Usage

- Apply `.tres` materials directly to meshes for instant blueprint visuals.  
- Use `.png` textures if you want to customize or build your own materials.  
- Mix white and blue variants to distinguish between different prototype elements.
